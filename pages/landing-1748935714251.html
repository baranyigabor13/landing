BEYOND AUTOMATION

Necessary & Non-Automatable Tasks in a B2B SAAS Copywriting Company

Identify the critical human-centric tasks essential for strategic growth and success, even with AI-powered tools.

Human Essentials in an AI World

These tasks demand human critical thinking, empathy, subjective judgment, negotiation, ethical considerations, and complex human interaction.

01

Strategic Planning & Corporate Governance

  • Defining and communicating the company's long-term vision, mission, and values.
  • Analyzing long-term market trends and setting strategic directions based on foresight, not just data aggregation.
  • Determining and refining competitive market positioning and unique value proposition.
  • Making complex, high-stakes decisions in uncertain environments (e.g., acquisition, market entry).
  • Consciously building, shaping, and maintaining corporate culture and employee inspiration.
  • Selecting, building, and nurturing strategic partnerships based on trust and long-term vision.
  • Strategic allocation of company resources (capital, talent, technology) based on priorities and risk appetite.
02

Complex Problem Solving & Innovation (Product & AI R&D)

  • Conceptual planning for the next generation of the AI model (new capabilities, handling nuance, tone).
  • Subjectively evaluating the *quality* of AI-generated texts (impact, persuasion, brand voice) and setting refinement directions.
  • Inventing new, innovative use cases or features for the software based on market needs and creative problem-solving.
  • Developing methods to identify and fix *undesired* behaviors in AI output (bias, inaccuracy, offensive content).
  • Diagnosing and solving unique, non-routine technical problems requiring high-level human expertise and analytical thought.
  • Strategic planning and refinement of the User Experience (UX) for intuitive AI tool interaction.
  • Researching and evaluating experimental AI models or approaches with uncertain outcomes, driven by curiosity and intuition.
03

Sales, Business Development & Complex Negotiation

  • Understanding a potential B2B client's *real*, deep business challenges and proposing tailored SAAS solutions.
  • Building trust and long-term relationships with B2B decision-makers, often over extended periods.
  • Complex negotiation of custom enterprise contracts, terms, and pricing, balancing legal, financial, and business aspects.
  • Handling disagreements and objections during the sales process, often rooted in human factors (fear of change, internal politics).
  • Identifying new, emerging B2B segments or industries and developing approach strategies based on market intuition.
  • Selecting strategic sales channels (resellers, affiliates) and formulating partnership terms.
  • Presenting the SAAS value proposition authentically, positioning AI as a complement, not replacement, for the human copywriter.
04

Customer Relationships, Success & Strategic Account Management

  • Resolving complex customer issues involving technical problems alongside human factors (frustration, negative experience).
  • Ensuring the long-term success of large, strategic B2B clients by deeply understanding evolving needs and building trust.
  • *Deeply* understanding customer feedback (beyond data), especially subjective experiences ("not creative enough," "misses the tone").
  • Handling customer crises (e.g., a failed campaign blamed on the software) with empathy, fast problem-solving, and crisis communication.
  • Managing varying needs and concerns of different stakeholders within a client organization (marketing, IT, legal).
  • Proactively suggesting ways for clients to achieve *even better* results with the SAAS, based on their specific business goals.
  • Compiling customer success stories and case studies that genuinely tell the client's success narrative.
05

Human Resources & Team Leadership

  • Hiring employees, evaluating cultural fit, motivation, and long-term potential beyond measurable skills.
  • Conducting performance reviews and providing constructive feedback to develop and motivate individuals.
  • Managing and mediating conflicts within teams, facilitating solutions with empathy and fairness.
  • Motivating and inspiring employees, maintaining team spirit especially during challenging times.
  • Mentoring and coaching individuals, supporting personal career paths with empathy and tailored advice.
  • Consciously building and maintaining a diverse and inclusive work environment.
  • Recognizing signs of workplace stress or burnout in employees and providing personalized support.
06

Marketing Strategy & Branding (Internal)

  • Defining and communicating the SAAS company's own brand identity, tone of voice, and visual style.
  • Creatively planning complex B2B marketing campaigns that emotionally resonate with the target audience.
  • Crafting website copy and landing page messaging that effectively communicates value and builds trust.
  • Developing PR strategy and managing media relations through human connection.
  • Hosting webinars, giving presentations, and other public appearances that involve personal and authentic communication.
  • Developing a social media strategy that reflects the brand and generates dialogue with the B2B audience.
  • Developing a content marketing strategy (blog posts, whitepapers, e-books) that educates and influences, not just sells.
07

Legal, Regulatory & Ethical Decision-Making

  • Interpreting, negotiating, and executing complex legal contracts (SAAS licenses, data processing agreements).
  • Continuously monitoring, interpreting, and adapting to data privacy (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) and advertising regulations.
  • Developing ethical guidelines for the AI regarding content it *must not* generate (discriminatory, misleading, manipulative).
  • Handling potential copyright or intellectual property issues related to AI-generated content.
  • Managing data security incidents or breaches, including communication with affected parties and authorities.
  • Handling labor law issues and resolving employee disputes from a legal perspective.
  • Developing and executing the company's Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) strategy.
08

Specialized Expert Evaluation & Subjective Judgment

  • Final expert review of AI-generated copy suggestions for *human*, *contextual*, and *creative* quality.
  • Subjectively evaluating and annotating data quality used for AI model training or fine-tuning to ensure desired *creative* or *impactful* language.
  • Developing new "prompts" or input parameters for the AI based on deep knowledge of marketing psychology and the target audience.
  • Deeper *interpretation* of A/B test results for AI-generated copy, going beyond numbers to understand *why* something worked.
  • Providing consultation to clients on how to *best use* the AI tool to achieve *their specific*, unique advertising goals.
  • Human review of potential ethical or legal risks in software-generated copy for high-stakes campaigns or industries.
  • Subjective evaluation of creative directions suggested by the software within a specific campaign context (emotional fit, resonance).
09

Crisis Management & Adaptation

  • Communicating and handling unexpected technical issues (e.g., major outage) with customers with empathy and transparency.
  • Strategically and operationally reacting to sudden, unforeseen changes in the market or regulatory environment.
  • Managing negative publicity or media attention and protecting the company's reputation.
  • Responding to labor market changes or unexpected personnel crises (e.g., key employee departure) and managing the human side.
